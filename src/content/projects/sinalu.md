SinaLu is an educational iOS app designed to make learning **Brazilian Sign Language (Libras)** approachable for children. Players accompany **Lu** on a journey to complete a magical book by learning new signs through games, exploration, and interactive activities.

## Features

- Follow Lu through a world filled with themed levels and new vocabulary.
- Learn Libras through animated lessons and interactive activities.
- Practice signs using the device camera or reinforce learning with quizzes.
- Build a personal sign dictionary to revisit everything learned.
- Express creativity in a drawing area connected to the educational content.
- Progress through a gamified world map that introduces concepts step by step.
