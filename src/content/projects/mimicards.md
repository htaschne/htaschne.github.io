Mimicards is my take on digital flashcards: fast, lightweight, and entirely owned by the user.

Instead of locking study material behind accounts or cloud platforms, Mimicards keeps decks as simple files that can be stored locally, synced with Google Drive, or shared directly with friends.

The goal is to make studying feel frictionless while giving users complete control over their data.

## Vision

Mimicards is built around a few core principles:

- Study should be simple.
- Cloud sync should be optional.
- Decks should belong to the user.
- Learning tools should feel clear and lightweight.

Future plans include:

- Markdown-powered cards.
- Image support.
- Shared deck libraries.
- Spaced repetition improvements.
- Desktop and mobile clients.

## Tech

- React
- TypeScript
- Electron
- React Native
- Google Drive integration

Mimicards is currently under active development.
